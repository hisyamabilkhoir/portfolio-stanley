Seperti Sebuah Nama: A Stepping Stone to International Distribution

By Kaisar Stanley Eleazar  •  4 Min Read

This is where I first learned to gather the courage to distribute my debut short documentary film to various film festivals, ranging from national to international scales.

Although it started with many failures and rejection after rejection from every film festival I applied to, thank God the universe eventually gave me a gift. My crew and I finally found several film festivals willing to screen this short documentary.

I was also lucky to work with a crew of my close friends for the first time, especially the director, who remains a close friend to this day.

"Through 'Seperti Sebuah Nama', I found my first stepping stone to be bolder in distributing the films I produce."

Additionally, the short film 'Seperti Sebuah Nama' was the first witness to my success in distributing my work to an international film platform called Viddsee. FYI, none of my previous films had ever entered any festival, but 'Seperti Sebuah Nama' became my first stepping stone to be bolder in distributing the films I produce.
