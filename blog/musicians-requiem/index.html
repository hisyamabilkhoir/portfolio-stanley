A Musician’s Requiem: A Cinematic Love Letter

By Kaisar Stanley Eleazar  •  3 Min Read

A Short Fiction Musical Drama film that I developed over a full year in 2025 with my close middle school friend.

Here, I experienced for the first time being a truly serious producer, managing the Pre-Production workflow that I led until the film was successfully distributed to international festivals abroad the following year.

"The Short Fiction Musical Drama titled A Musician’s Requiem is my love letter to the most beautiful ex-partner I had at that time."

FYI, this project was not just an ordinary assignment or production, but a deep emotional manifestation. Balancing musical elements with a fictional drama narrative presented its own challenges in the production process, but the final result became one of my most personal works.

Kaisar Stanley
